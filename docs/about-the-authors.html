About the authors

Carrie Wright is an Assistant Scientist in the Department of Biostatistics at the Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health (JHSPH). Dr. Wright is a member of the Johns Hopkins Data Science Lab (DaSL), the Open Case Studies team, and the Informatics Technology for Cancer Research (ITCR) Training Network (ITN). Dr. Wright’s work is focused on innovating ways to make data science and computational biology more accessible to audiences of varied computational experience. She is particularly interested in practices to enhance flexibility, rigor, reproducibility, and ethical consideration in scientific and medical research. You can learn more about Dr.Wright at https://carriewright11.github.io/.
